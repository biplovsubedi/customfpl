General Rules

Scoring

All the scoring will be done in this web application (Please message in the discord channel if you find some errors)

Non Anti Teams

Teams that are playing regular fantasy (score lot of points, start premiums) will be kicked out of the league.

Dead Teams

Teams that are not active for a few Gameweeks will be kicked out of the league.

Repeated Offense

Teams that regularly break rules (regular bank penalty, etc) will be kicked out of the league.

Scoring Method

Inactive Player Penalty

At the end of the week after (auto-subs have been made) if you have any players in your final lineup that played 0 minutes , a penalty of +9 points will be applied to each inactive player in your XI.

Bank penalty

At no time can your team have more than £3.0 in the bank. Having a squad with more than £3.0 in the bank will will result in a +25 points penalty for the week. Repeat offenders will be kicked out of the league.

Hits

Normal FPL transfer rules apply, if you take a hit it will be added to your points and not subtracted.

C/VC Failure to play

If your Captain or Vice Captain fails to play you will receive a +15 points penalty to your gameweek score.

Chip Rules

All chips (wildcard optional) must be used by the end of GW38. Failure to use your chips will result in a +25 points penalty per chip to be applied to your GW38 score. Also, you won't be eligible to will the league or the cup.

Bench Boost Explained

When using the bench boost chip, scoring will be done on 15 of your players. So if you have 11 players that actually play but 4 on your bench that register 0 minutes you will get inactive player penalty to the 4 players on your bench. So when you decide to use BB make sure everyone on your team actually gets minutes
